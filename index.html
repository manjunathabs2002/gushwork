Frequently Asked Questions

What are HDPE pipes used for?

HDPE pipes are used for water supply, drainage systems, gas distribution, mining, agriculture, and industrial applications due to their flexibility, durability, and resistance to chemicals.

How long do HDPE pipes last?

Gushwork HDPE pipes are engineered to last 50+ years when properly installed and maintained under normal operating conditions, significantly outlasting traditional alternatives.

Are HDPE pipes environmentally friendly?

Yes, our HDPE pipes are fully recyclable and manufactured using sustainable processes. They reduce environmental impact and can be repurposed at end-of-life.

What makes your pipes different from competitors?

Gushwork pipes feature superior impact resistance, better chemical compatibility, enhanced UV protection, and competitive pricing backed by 20+ years of industry expertise.

Do you offer custom sizes and specifications?

Yes, we provide customization options for specific project requirements. Contact our sales team to discuss your custom specifications and requirements.

What is your installation warranty?

All Gushwork products come with comprehensive warranties. Installation guidelines and technical support are available to ensure proper installation and warranty coverage.
